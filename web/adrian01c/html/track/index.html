Did Romania melt the Russian tanks with a laser in 1968?

This was a reprisal prompted by Ceausescu’s defiant stance, particularly with regard to the Warsaw Pact invasion of Czechoslovakia earlier that year. That is when, supposedly, a top secret super powerful laser sprung into action, melting/vaporizing several tanks and aircraft, scaring the Soviets shitless and dissuading them from their belligerent intentions.
